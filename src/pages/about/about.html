About

A warm welcome, and thank you for joining the Walking in Hózhó community!

We are a team of psychologists, computer scientists and clinicians in Flagstaff, Arizona, who share a passion for type 2 diabetes prevention. We have joined together to serve the Navajo community to develop the Walking in Hózhó mobile platform.

With Walking in Hózhó, we seek to empower and support Navajo individuals in their journey toward harmony, beauty, and well-being. Our mission is to better understand your experiences with eating and physical activity, in order to support your healthy choices in a personally meaningful way. Thank you for joining us, and do not hesitate to contact us with any questions or comments (natalia.dmitrieva@nau.edu).
